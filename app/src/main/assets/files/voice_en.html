Supported commands

  • open calendar;
  • open settings;
  • open locations;
  • volume settings;
  • open application (only from widget);
  • open help;
  • new birthday;
  • new reminder;

() - in parentheses You can change text

** - it optional element

*calendar* - if You want to synchronize reminder with calendar (if enabled in settings)


Add reminder with repeating

Constructor: every (х) (days, weeks, months, years) (task) *at (х) hours (х) minutes* *calendar*

Example: Every 3 days check project readiness


Add reminder after specified time

Constructor: after (х) (hours, minutes, days, weeks, months, years) (task), *remind (х) (hours, minutes, days, weeks, months, years) before* *calendar*

Example: After 2 weeks wife's birthday, reminds 2 days before


Add reminders by date and time

Constructor: (task) (month) (х) at (х) hours (х) minutes *calendar*

Example: (Cinema) January 27 at 20 hours 30 minutes

* - If specified, past time, the reminder will be automatically added for next year


Add reminder for an indefinite time

Times of day You can change in "Settings > Voice control > Times of day"

Constructor: (task) *in next (day of week)* morning/afternoon/tonight/night *calendar*

Constructor: (task) *tomorrow* morning/afternoon/tonight/night *calendar*

Example: (Check mail) tomorrow at morning

Example: (Cinema) tomorrow tonight

Example: (Cinema) in next (friday) tonight

Example: (Pay utilities)in next (monday) in the afternoon


Add reminder by time

Constructor: remind (task) at (х) time *tomorrow* *calendar*

Example: Remind (check mail) at (16) hours

Example: Remind (check mail) at (10) hours tomorrow, calendar


Add reminder after specified time

Constructor: remind (task) after (х) hours/minutes *tomorrow* *calendar*

Example: Rremind (check mail) after (30) minutes

Example: Remind (check mail) after (2) hours, calendar


Add reminder by weekdays

Constructor:(task) in (weekdays) at (х) hours

Example: (Wake up) in (monday, tuesday and thursday) at (16) hours


Add call reminder

Constructor:call (contact) at (х) hours *tomorrow* *calendar*

Example: Call (Mr Smith) at (16) hour, calendar


Add send message reminder

Constructor:send (text) to (contact) at (х) hours *tomorrow* *calendar*

Example: Send (whats up) to (Mr Smith) at (13) hour, calendar


Add note

Constructor:note (text)

Example: Note (buy milk)


Show birthdays

Constructor:show birthdays for next (x) days

Example: Show birthdays for next (10) days
